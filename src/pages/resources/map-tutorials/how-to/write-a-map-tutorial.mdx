# Hi 👋

Thank you for considering writing an NGC Map Tutorial. Our aim is to provide the cartographic community with valuable content, and we would greatly
appreciate your contribution. -- Anyone can submit an NGC Map Tutorial!

## 1. Tell us about the topic

Join our Discord server, and the #ngc-map-tutorials channel,
or write an E-mail to xxx@xxx.xx: tell us about the topic on which you want to
write! We will check that your topic:

- is related to map-making
- aligns with our Code of Conduct
- has not already been covered in an NGC Map Tutorial

If we confirm, you can start writing!

## 2. Write the tutorial

You should write your tutorial using the template (see next page).

## 3. Submit your tutorial for review

Once you are finished, send us your draft (preferably via Discord
in a private group message or via E-mail). We will ask someone from a related
field to review your draft to ensure the quality of the NGC Map Tutorials. The
reviewer might ask you to change some parts of your tutorial.

Additionally, we will check that your tutorial follows the Code of Conduct and that the images and content you use are correctly credited.

We then send you the suggestions for modification. The whole reviewing process can take several weeks.

## 4. We publish your tutorial!

Once everything is ready, we will publish your tutorial
on the NGC website! We will also advertise it in a LinkedIn post, based on
what you will share in your template. If you have any questions or concerns,
you can send messages in the #map-tutorials channel of the Discord server.
